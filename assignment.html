Assignment: Exploring MicroGPT with Your Own Dataset

In this assignment, you will select a dataset of your choice, process it using the MicroGPT framework, and analyze the results. This exercise is designed to help you understand how transformer-based models can be applied to real-world data and to encourage critical thinking about model performance and limitations.

Step 1: Choose a Dataset

Pick a dataset that interests you. Here are some ideas to get you started:

  • Text Data: Movie reviews, news articles, song lyrics, tweets, product reviews, or Reddit comments.
  • Tabular Data: Sports statistics, weather records, financial data, or survey results (convert to text if needed).
  • Custom Data: Your own writing, emails (anonymized), or any collection of text you find interesting.

Tip: Try to pick a dataset with at least a few hundred lines of text for meaningful results.

Step 2: Run MicroGPT

  1. Prepare your dataset in a format compatible with MicroGPT (e.g., plain text, one sample per line).
  2. Follow the instructions in microgpt_walkthrough.md to process your dataset through MicroGPT.
  3. Save your results and any generated outputs for analysis.

Step 3: Analyze and Report

Write a short report (1-2 pages) addressing the following points:

  • Dataset Description: What dataset did you choose and why?
  • Preprocessing: What steps did you take to prepare the data?
  • Model Results: Summarize the outputs from MicroGPT. What patterns or interesting results did you observe?
  • Model Performance: How well did MicroGPT handle your dataset? Were there any surprising successes or failures?
  • Limitations: What limitations did you notice in the model or your approach?
  • Improvements: Suggest ways you might improve the results (e.g., more data, different preprocessing, model tweaks).

Include code snippets, output samples, and visualizations if helpful.

Submission

  • Your report (PDF or DOCX)
  • Your dataset (or a sample, if large)
  • Any code or scripts you used

Submit your work via the course portal by the deadline.
